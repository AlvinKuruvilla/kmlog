How the Tasks Page Works

On the following tasks page, you will see a series of videos. Once you've finished watching a video, click the 'Next Task' button to post your thoughts on the social media platform. Make sure once your done you press the "Post" button. Now navigate back to the tasks page, and repeat the process.

You will have to make a total of 18 posts (3 videos on 3 platforms, with 2 complete cycles = 18). Since the videos repeat, you don't have to re-watch them unless you choose to do so.

Are you ready? Begin!
